A wrapper around [THREE.LOD](https://threejs.org/docs/#api/en/objects/LOD) (Level of detail).

```jsx
<Detailed distances={[0, 10, 20]} {...props}>
  <mesh geometry={highDetail} />
  <mesh geometry={mediumDetail} />
  <mesh geometry={lowDetail} />
</Detailed>
```
